Why Choose Green Dolphin?

We take pride in our work because of our professional services and because we use cleaning detergents supplied by Vertec Biosolvents. This is a company that specializes in the development and production of environmentally friendly solvents and cleaning products. Their focus is on creating innovative solutions that are safer for both human health and the environment, without compromising on performance. Their products are designed to replace traditional Sulphur -based solvents with bio-based alternatives, offering a more sustainable choice for various industries such as cleaning, coatings, adhesives, and more. Vertec Biosolvents is committed to providing effective and sustainable solutions that align with the growing demand for environmentally conscious products.
