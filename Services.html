Services

Green Dolphin offers a comprehensive range of exterior cleaning services tailored to meet your specific needs. From roof and gutter cleaning to window and house siding cleaning, we handle it all with expertise and precision. Our dedicated team is equipped to tackle challenging projects, including driveway and sidewalk cleaning, as well as backyard revitalization. With our meticulous attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction, you can trust Green Dolphin to transform and enhance the appearance of your property.

Roof Cleaning

Our roof cleaning process begins with a thorough inspection to assess the condition and identify any issues. Then, we broom or air blow the moss, algae and other dirts on roof after which we treat the whole roof with Vertec bio-solvents detergents for a guarantied clean roof for atleast 10 to 12 months.

Gutter Cleaning

During our gutter cleaning process, we start by inspecting the gutters for debris and blockages. We then utilize professional tools and techniques to remove leaves, twigs, and other buildup, ensuring proper water flow and preventing potential damage to your property.

Soffit Cleaning

Our process involves a thorough inspection of the soffits to identify dirt, grime, or any signs of damage. We then utilize specialized cleaning techniques and eco-friendly solutions to effectively remove debris and restore the soffits' original shine and cleanliness, enhancing the overall appearance of your property.

House Sides Wash

Our house siding wash process begins with a careful examination of the siding's condition and material. We then employ safe and effective cleaning methods, such as soft washing or pressure washing, along with appropriate detergents, to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and other contaminants. This meticulous approach ensures a thorough and revitalizing clean, leaving your house siding looking fresh and vibrant.

Window Cleaning

When it comes to window cleaning, we take pride in delivering streak-free and crystal-clear results. Our process involves using professional-grade tools and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to remove dirt, smudges, and grime from both the interior and exterior surfaces of the windows. With our meticulous attention to detail and specialized techniques, we ensure that your windows are left sparkling, allowing natural light to illuminate your space and providing a clear view of the outside world.

Pressure washing of Driveway, Sidewalk and Backyard

Our pressure washing services for driveways, sidewalks, and backyards are designed to restore the cleanliness and enhance the overall appearance of these surfaces. Using powerful pressure washing equipment, we remove embedded dirt, grime, oil stains, and other unsightly contaminants. Our skilled technicians employ proper techniques to ensure thorough and even cleaning, rejuvenating these areas and leaving them looking fresh, vibrant, and inviting.
